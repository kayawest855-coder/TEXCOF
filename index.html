"WELCOME TO TEXAS COFFEE" : THE BEST IN THE WEST!

Saddle up and mosey on over to Texas Coffee, where the West meets its match in the world of coffee! Our shop is dedicated to serving up the best cups in the Lone Star State, with a passion for quality, community, and a whole lot of Texas pride.

RECIPIES

From classic favorites to bold new creations, our menu is designed to satisfy any coffee craving. Try our signature drinks, like the "Cinnamon Dolce Latte" or the "Coffee Granita" , or stick with a good ol' fashioned cup of Caramel Macchiato . Whatever your taste, we've got you covered.

ICED COFFEE

Vanilla Sweet Cream Cold Brew

Combine cold brew coffee, vanilla syrup, and sweet cream over ice.

Caramel Macchiato

Mix espresso, vanilla syrup, and caramel sauce over ice, topped with whipped cream.

HOT COFFEE

Cinnamon Dolce Latte

Combine espresso, steamed milk, and cinnamon syrup, topped with whipped cream.

Gingerbread Latte

Combine espresso, steamed milk, and cinnamon syrup, topped with whipped cream.

SPECIALTY COFFEE

Coffee Tiramisu

Layer coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, and cocoa powder.

Coffee Granita

Freeze coffee, scrape with a fork, and serve over ice.

ABOUT US

From the dusty trails of El Paso to the cowboy boots of Amarillo, Texas has a rich history of rugged individualism and warm hospitality. At Texas Coffee, we're committed to carrying on that tradition, one cup at a time. Our expert baristas are dedicated to crafting drinks that are as smooth as a Texas summer breeze and as bold as a West Texas sunset.
